About Voice Assistant

Our voice assistant is designed to help you navigate through various mental health resources. It uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing to understand and respond to your queries.

Features include:

  • Speech-to-text recognition for seamless interaction.
  • Natural language understanding to provide accurate responses.
  • Integration with mental health resources and support tools.
  • Ability to track and analyze common queries for ongoing improvement.
