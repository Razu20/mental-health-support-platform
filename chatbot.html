How It Works

Our AI chatbot uses advanced natural language processing to understand and respond to your queries. It is designed to provide support, answer questions, and offer resources based on your needs.

To start a conversation, simply type your question or concern in the chat interface. Our chatbot will respond with helpful information and suggestions. If you need more personalized assistance, you can also schedule a session with a mental health professional through our platform.
